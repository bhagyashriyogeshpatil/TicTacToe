How to play

  • Be the first to get three of your marks in a row.
  • The game typically starts with an empty grid.
  • Players take turns marking empty cells with their symbol.
  • X marks for one player, O marks for the other.
  • Get three marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The game ends when either a player wins or the game is a draw.
Enjoy!
